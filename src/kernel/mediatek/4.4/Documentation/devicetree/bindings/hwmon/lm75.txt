* LM75 thermometer.

Optional properties:
- temp_check_low_limit: The lower limit(hyst) for the temp sensor temperature check.
- temp_check_high_limit: The higher limit for the temp sensor temperature check.

Example:

temperature-sensor@0 {
		compatible = "lm75,tmp75";
		reg = <0>;
		temp_check_low_limit = <60000>
		temp_check_high_limit = <87000>
};
